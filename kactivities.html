Environmental tournament


Living in a rural area where many in my community are skeptical about climate change, I initiated this project to educate and raise awareness through soccer, a beloved sport. So far, we have successfully organized three tournaments. Soccer provides an effective platform to gather the community and educate them on climate change and conservation.


Mission


Our mission is to educate underserved rural populations on confronting climate-related health threats and achieving environmental justice through pollution reduction and sustainable practices. By engaging with the community, we aim to understand and dispel myths about climate change, fostering a collective effort for long-lasting solutions. We strive to transform societal values from profit-centric to sustainability-focused, meeting essential human needs. Let's seize every opportunity to raise awareness and amplify the message of climate change. The time to act is now.
