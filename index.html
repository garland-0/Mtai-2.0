KEVIN MTAI

Climate Activist , Speaker, Co-founder Of KEAN And Communication Coordinator

Kevin Mtai, a passionate climate activist and compelling speaker,
is not just the Co-founder of the Kheri Environment Action Network (KEAN),
a non-profit organization committed to ecosystem restoration and community
education on climate action since 2020, but also the dedicated Communication
Coordinator at the Loss and Damage Youth Coalition, an ambassador to the United Nations OCHA,
and a youth Champion member for the Fossil Fuel Non-Proliferation Treaty Initiative.
An active member and campaigner at Climate Live and Fridays for Future International,
his influential impact resonates globally. Kevin's achievements include recognition
from esteemed media outlets like BBC, CNN, and Forbes, and active participation in
major global events and conferences, including COPs.
In addition to his advocacy efforts, Kevin collaborates with Greenhouse Communication,
showcasing his skills as a videographer and photographer during COPs. Furthermore,
he serves on the Ingka Young Leader Board and volunteers at Earth Uprising International
as the Global Development Director. His multifaceted contributions underscore his commitment
to fostering positive change on a global scale.
